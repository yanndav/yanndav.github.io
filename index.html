ecolometrics

Open knowledge hub on ecological transitionsUnder development

The objective of ecolometrics is to provide rapide access to centralised and harmonised data on ecological issues and solutions. Users will be able to create their own dashboards as well as to contribute to the tool by signaling unreferenced datasets. In the middle run it will improve experience with the inclusion of new features such as academic articles suggestion, and meta-analyses policies impact evaluation. The project builds on the two master's thesis I am writing to validate my degrees.
